My name is Inês, born and raised in Porto - Portugal, and I'm a designer generalist currently working at Porto4Ageing. As a 90's baby, I have a great appreciation for the Internet, I owe part of my education to online courses, workshops, youtube tutorials and even Tiktoks. I also have a Fine Arts BA and I'm currently a Multimedia Communication Master’s student researching on Human-Centered design and cocreation processes, for the development of a more inclusive, equitable and accessible future. My multidisciplinary education as allowed me to explore many fields of study and learn from different people. I like to believe that it makes me a more versatile and empathetic designer. When I'm not designing, you can find me practicing my coding skills, listening to funny podcasts, walking in nature and taking selfies with photogenic cows.
